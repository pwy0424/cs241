MiniMP for CS 241 Discussion Sections on Sept 19, 2013

Introduction

In this MiniMP, you will be working on a couple of pointer arithmetic problems by implementing the best-fit search algorithm in multiple different ways; The simplest of which has been implemented for you in the best_fit(). However, you will need to implement the other two, i.e. best_fit_ptr() and best_fit_ptr2()

You should look at ptr_acrobats.c in your ds3 folder.

Compile and Run

To compile:
$ make clean
$ make
To run:
$ ./ptr_acrobats
